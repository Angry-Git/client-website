About Us

Hi there! My name is Myra, and I live in Ringle, Wisconsin. I've always had a love for creating things with my hands and collecting the bits and pieces that tell stories from the past. What started as a personal hobbyâ€”making gifts for friends, gathering old paper, and sewing late into the night, slowly blossomed into something more.

This shop is a reflection of the things I treasure: meaningful details, forgotten treasures, and handmade items that serve a purpose while bringing a little beauty into the everyday. I find joy in turning fabric scraps into something useful, bundling vintage papers for journaling, and curating collections of ephemera that might otherwise be lost to time.

My handmade items like bowl cozies, clothespin bags, and zipper pouches are sewn with care and practicality in mind. I make things I actually use in my own home, and I love sharing them with others who appreciate both function and charm. Each piece is unique, and no two are ever quite the same.

On the vintage side, I spend hours at estate sales, antique shops, and thrift stores searching for old music sheets, postcards, books, buttons, and sewing patterns. Every item I find feels like a little piece of history worth passing on. I bundle these treasures so crafters, scrapbookers, and collectors can use them to create new stories of their own.

Thank you so much for supporting this little shop. Every order truly makes my day, and I'm so grateful to be able to share what I love with others. If you ever have questions, custom requests, or just want to say hello, I'd love to hear from you.
