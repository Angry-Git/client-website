Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed within 2-4 business days. Domestic shipping usually takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Tracking information will be provided once your item ships.

Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Yes! I accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original condition. Custom or personalized items may not be eligible for return unless they arrive damaged.

Are your items handmade or vintage?

I offer a mix of handmade goods crafted by me and curated vintage items. Each product listing will indicate whether it is handmade or vintage in the description.

Do you offer custom orders?

Absolutely! I'm happy to work with you on custom fabric patterns, sizes, or themed bundles. Please send me a message through the Contact page and we can discuss the details.

Can I request gift wrapping?

Yes, gift wrapping is available upon request for a small additional fee. Just leave a note during checkout or message me directly with your request.

Where do you source your vintage items?

I carefully curate vintage items from estate sales, antique markets, and local collections. I take pride in selecting pieces that are charming, useful, and full of character.
